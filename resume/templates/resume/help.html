Getting Started

Welcome to the AI Resume Generator! This guide helps you navigate and use our features effectively.

  • Sign up or log in to your account.
  • Fill in your personal, educational, and professional details.
  • Choose your preferred resume template.
  • Click Generate Resume to preview and download your professional resume.

Common FAQs

1. How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page → click “Forgot Password” → follow the email instructions to set a new password.

2. Can I edit my resume after generating it?

Yes, log in and click “Edit Resume” from your dashboard to make updates.

3. How can I contact support?

You can email us at support@resumeai.com for technical or account help.

Tips for Better Resumes

  • Use short, action-driven bullet points.
  • Keep your resume under 2 pages.
  • Highlight measurable achievements.
  • Choose a clean and readable template.

Still Need Help?
